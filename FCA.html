Developing and delivering World Class Training & Consulting

Managing the training and consulting process at FCA and becoming a benchmark for external companies

In practice, this consists of:

  • Developing effective training solutions to increase manufacturing skills and accelerating the spread of a culture of improvement at FCA plants
  • Training and coaching FCA’s Internal Trainers
  • Standardizing World Class training packages, incorporating learning solutions and best practices developed on the factory floor
  • Sharing the FCA approach with suppliers and WCM Association partners
